Privacy Policy

This app does not collect any personal data or use third-party analytics or tracking services.

The only data collected is when you voluntarily submit a high score for leaderboard purposes after completing a level. In that case, the following information is stored:

  • Your entered name
  • Your score
  • The date of submission

If your score does not make it into the overall top 10 for the completed level, it will be automatically deleted after one month.

If you wish to have your submitted data removed, please email thomas.dengiz@yahoo.de.
